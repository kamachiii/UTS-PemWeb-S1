Frequently Asked Questions

What are the pharmacy's operating hours?

Our pharmacy operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, to ensure you have access to medications and health products anytime you need them.

Do you offer home delivery services for medicines?

Yes, we offer home delivery services. Simply place your order via our website or contact us through WhatsApp, and we’ll ensure your medications are delivered to your doorstep promptly.

Can I consult a pharmacist for my health issues?

Absolutely! Our licensed pharmacists are available 24/7 to provide professional advice regarding your medications and health concerns. You can visit us in-store or contact us online for a consultation.

Do you sell over-the-counter (OTC) medicines?

Yes, we stock a wide range of over-the-counter medicines, including remedies for colds, allergies, pain relief, vitamins, and supplements. No prescription is required for these items.

What types of payment methods are accepted?

We accept a variety of payment methods for your convenience, including cash, debit/credit cards, and e-wallets like GoPay, OVO, and Dana.

Can I return or exchange medicines?

Medicines can only be returned or exchanged if they are unopened, undamaged, and accompanied by the original receipt. However, prescription medications are non-returnable due to safety regulations.
